The International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) Red List of Threatened Species serves as the world's most comprehensive source of information on the global extinction risk status of animal, fungal, and plant species. Its main objective is to guide and promote biodiversity conservation actions.

WHAT IS SPECIES EXTINCTION?

Species extinction occurs when a species ceases to exist completely, with no living specimens, making its reproduction or survival impossible....
